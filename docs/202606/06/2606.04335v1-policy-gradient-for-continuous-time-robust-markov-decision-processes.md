## Abstract
The framework of robust Markov decision processes (RMDPs) allows the design of reinforcement learning agents that satisfy performance guarantees under worst-case transition dynamics. Traditional RMDPs consider discrete-time dynamics and recently, sample-efficient policy gradient algorithms have been considered in this context. This paper investigates policy gradient algorithms within a continuous-time RMDP framework. Policy gradients and adversarial gradients are derived using pathwise and adjoint-based formulas for stochastic and ordinary differential equations. We propose double-loop optimisers to obtain linear convergence in the oracle-based setting and an $\tilde{\mathcal{O}}(\frac{1}{ε^2})$ sample complexity in the sample-based setting in an analysis which also derives novel tools for the framework of undiscounted total cost MDPs. Additionally, we propose mean-field optimisers as distributional optimisers with an $\tilde{\mathcal{O}}(\frac{1}{K})$ oracle-based convergence rate and an $\tilde{\mathcal{O}}(\frac{N^2}ε)$ sample complexity under $N$-particle approximation. The effectiveness of continuous-time policy gradient algorithms is confirmed for both optimisers on continuous-time RMDPs with neural ordinary differential equation dynamics.

## 2. 论文方法论

### 2.1 核心思想

将RMDP的min-max优化问题扩展到连续时间设定：  
$$\min_\theta \max_{\xi \in \Xi} J(\theta, \xi)$$
其中 $J(\theta, \xi)$ 为期望累计成本，$\xi$ 参数化转移动态的不确定性。交替对策略参数 $\theta$ 梯度下降、对对抗参数 $\xi$ 梯度上升。

### 2.2 关键技术细节

#### 2.2.1 路径式策略梯度（Pathwise Policy Gradient）
- **策略敏感性过程**：$z_t := \nabla_\theta x_t$，满足线性ODE  
  $$\dot{z}_t = \nabla_x f_\xi(t,x_t,u_t) z_t + \nabla_u f_\xi(t,x_t,u_t)[\nabla_x \mu_\theta(t,x_t) z_t + \nabla_\theta \mu_\theta(t,x_t)]$$
- **路径梯度公式**（确定性策略）：  
  $$\nabla_\theta J(\theta,\xi) = E\left[\int_0^T (\nabla_u r \cdot (\nabla_x \mu_\theta \cdot z_t + \nabla_\theta \mu_\theta) + \nabla_x r \cdot z_t) dt + \nabla_x R(x_T) \cdot z_T\right]$$
- **对抗梯度公式**：引入对抗敏感性 $y_t := \nabla_\xi x_t$，得到类似结构。

#### 2.2.2 伴随式策略梯度（Adjoint-based Policy Gradient）
- **伴随方程**（逆向求解）：  
  $$-\dot{p}_t = \nabla_x r + \nabla_x f_\xi^\top p_t, \quad p_T = \nabla_x R(x_T)$$
- **确定性策略梯度**：  
  $$\nabla_\theta J(\theta,\xi) = E\left[\int_0^T (\nabla_\theta \mu_\theta + \nabla_x \mu_\theta z_t)^\top \nabla_u H(t,x_t,u_t,p_t) dt\right]$$
- **随机策略梯度**：基于对数导数形式  
  $$\nabla_\theta J(\theta,\xi) = E\left[\int_0^T (\nabla_\theta \log \pi_\theta + \nabla_x \log \pi \cdot z_t)^\top Q(t,x_t,u_t) dt\right]$$
- **对抗梯度**：  
  $$\nabla_\xi J(\theta,\xi) = E\left[\int_0^T p_t^\top \nabla_\xi f_\xi(t,x_t,u_t) dt\right]$$

#### 2.2.3 SDE（随机微分方程）扩展
- 动力学：$dX_t = b_\xi(t,X_t,U_t)dt + \sigma_\xi(t,X_t,U_t)dW_t$
- 引入双伴随态 $(P_t, Q_t)$，其中 $Q_t$ 捕获曲率-噪声耦合
- 策略/对抗梯度形式类似ODE但含额外迹项

#### 2.2.4 离散化误差分析
- 证明Euler离散化下状态误差为 $O(h)$（$h$ 为时间步长）
- 策略/对抗梯度整体误差同样为 $O(h)$

### 2.3 算法流程

#### 双环优化器（Algorithm 2）
1. 对每个宏观迭代 $k$：
   - 采样轨迹，估计 $\nabla_\theta J(\theta_{k-1}, \xi_{k-1})$
   - 策略更新：$\theta_k \leftarrow \theta_{k-1} - \eta_1 \nabla_\theta J$
   - 内循环（对抗优化）：重复执行投影梯度上升直到接近最优
2. 最终输出 $\theta_K, \xi_K$

#### 均值场优化器（Algorithm 3, MFL-SDA）
- 在参数分布空间上优化：$\min_{\nu \in \mathcal{P}[\Theta]} \max_{\rho \in \mathcal{P}[\Xi]} G(\nu,\rho)$
- 使用 Wasserstein 梯度流的离散化形式，添加Langevin正则化项
- N粒子近似：梯度估计为 $\frac{1}{N}\sum_{j=1}^N \nabla_\theta J(\theta_i, \xi_j)$
