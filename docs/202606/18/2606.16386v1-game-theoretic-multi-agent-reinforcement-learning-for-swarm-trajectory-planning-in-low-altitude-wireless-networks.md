## Abstract
The Low-Altitude Economy (LAE) is rapidly expanding, giving rise to low-altitude wireless networks (LAWNs), where large-scale cellular-connected unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV) deployments support heterogeneous mission-critical applications over multi-cell ground base station (GBS) infrastructures. To ensure mission success, each UAV must jointly optimize communication throughput and mission completion efficiency. In fifth-generation (5G) new radio (NR) systems, the equal resource block (RB) allocation policy induces strong strategic coupling among UAV trajectories: when a UAV enters a GBS cell, it reduces the RB share available to all co-served UAVs, thereby altering their achievable rates and trajectory incentives through shared wireless resources. Existing studies either ignore this coupling or focus on single-cell infrastructure, leaving the multi-cell, congestion-aware UAV trajectory planning problem insufficiently addressed. To fill this gap, we formulate the problem as a cooperative stochastic congestion game with a communication-and-mission-aware utility function, and propose a centralized-training decentralized-execution multi-agent proximal policy optimization (CTDE-MAPPO) algorithm to maximize social welfare under multi-cell RB congestion. Simulation results show that the proposed method outperforms QMIX, independent Q-learning, and random baselines in terms of aggregate utility and mission success rate, while achieving stable convergence within practical training budgets.

## 2. 论文提出的方法论

### 核心思想

- 将多UAV轨迹规划问题**建模为合作随机拥塞博弈**（cooperative stochastic congestion game），利用博弈论显式捕获共享RB分配导致的拥塞外部性，并通过社会福利最大化表达集体目标。
- 采用**CTDE（集中训练分布式执行）架构**的MAPPO（多智能体近端策略优化）算法，兼顾训练阶段的全局信息利用与执行阶段的去中心化部署需求。

### 关键技术细节

#### (1) 通信模型

- **RB分配规则**：GBS $b$ 将 $K_b$ 个RB均等分配给当前关联的 $n_b(t)$ 架无人机，UAV $i$ 获得的RB数为 $\kappa_{b,i}(t) = \frac{K_b}{n_b(t)}$。
- **可达速率**：基于LoS（视距）主导的空地传播模型，速率计算为 $R_i(t) = \kappa_{b,i}(t) \cdot \Delta f \log_2\left(1 + \frac{P_{tx}\beta_0}{\sigma^2\|q_i(t)-g_{b_i(t)}\|^\alpha}\right)$。
- **最近GBS关联规则**：每架无人机关联至欧氏距离最近的地面基站。

#### (2) 效用函数设计

- **指数移动平均（EMA）速率** $\bar{R}_i(t) = (1-\beta)\bar{R}_i(t-1) + \beta R_i(t)$，捕获速率质量与时序一致性。
- **任务效率度量**：$\frac{T_{\min}^i}{T_i}$（最小飞行时间与实际完成时间之比），对不同起终点距离的 mission 公平归一化。
- **玩家效用（终端 payoff）**：$U_i = \hat{R}_i(T_i) \cdot \frac{T_{\min}^i}{T_i}$，其中 $\hat{R}_i(T_i)$ 为偏差校正后的终端EMA速率。采用**乘积形式**联合表征通信质量与任务完成效率。

#### (3) 奖励分解

为解决终端 payoff 导致的稀疏反馈问题，设计密集型分步奖励：
- **速率奖励**：$r_{\text{rate},i}(t) = \bar{R}_i(t+1) - \bar{R}_i(t)$，利用EMA增量的时序性促进通信质量而不直接鼓励更长轨迹。
- **导航塑形奖励**：$r_{\text{nav},i}(t) = \omega\left(\|q_i(t)-d_i\| - \|q_i(t+1)-d_i\|\right)$，向目标移动则正向奖励，远离则负向惩罚。
- **碰撞惩罚**：$r_{\text{col},i}(t) = -\rho_{\text{col}} \cdot \mathbf{1}[\exists j \neq i: q_j(t+1)=q_i(t+1)]$，避免物理冲突。
- **终端奖励**：$r_{\text{term},i}(t)$ 在成功到达目标时赋予 $U_i$，超时则惩罚 $-r_{\text{fail}}$。

#### (4) 网络架构与训练算法

- **全局状态** $x(t) = \{\{q_i(t), d_i, T_{\min}^i, \bar{R}_i(t)\}_{i \in \mathcal{N}}, \{n_b(t), g_b\}_{b \in \mathcal{B}}\}$：训练阶段中央 critic 可访问全部拥塞耦合信息，实现正确的信用分配。
- **本地观测** $o_i(t) = \{q_i(t), d_i, T_{\min}^i, \bar{R}_i(t), \{n_b(t), g_b\}_{b \in \mathcal{B}}\}$：执行阶段各无人机仅依据本地观测决策，$n_b(t)$ 作为拥塞充分统计量。
- **Actor网络**：两层MLP（256单元，ReLU），共享参数，softmax输出离散动作分布（$\|a\|_1 \leq v_{\max}$）。
- **Critic网络**：两层MLP（256单元，ReLU），输入全局状态 $x(t)$，估计折扣团队回报，采用价值归一化（value normalization）应对乘积效用引起的变幅奖励。
- **优势估计**：采用GAE（广义优势估计）$\hat{A}_{i,t} = \sum_{k=0}^{L-t-1}(\gamma\lambda_{\text{GAE}})^k \delta_{i,t+k}$。
- **策略更新**：PPO裁剪代理目标 $L_{\text{clip}}(\theta)$ + 熵正则项 $c_e H(\pi_\theta)$。
- **执行阶段**：丢弃中央 critic，各无人机依据本地观测独立行动，无需机间通信。
