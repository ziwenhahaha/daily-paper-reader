## 摘要
本文研究了结合符号回归 (SR) 的基于 Kolmogorov-Arnold 网络自编码器 (KAN-AE)，用于高能效信道编码。通过使用 SR，我们将 KAN-AE 转换为符号表达式，从而实现了低复杂度实现并提高了无线设备的能效。为了进一步提高效率，我们在 SR 过程中引入了一个新的非线性评分项，以帮助在可能的情况下选择复杂度较低的方程。通过数值模拟，我们证明了 KAN-AE 在与 SR 结合时，在提高能效的同时实现了具有竞争力的误块率 (BLER) 性能。我们使用提出的非线性指标对 KAN-AE 实现的能效进行评分，并将其与基于多层感知器的自编码器 (MLP-AE) 进行比较。实验表明，结合 SR 的 KAN-AE 消耗的能量比 MLP-AE 少 1.38 倍，这证明了 KAN-AE 是高能效深度学习信道编码的一个有前景的选择。
## Abstract
In this paper, we investigate Kolmogorov-Arnold network-based autoencoders (KAN-AEs) with symbolic regression (SR) for energy-efficient channel coding. By using SR, we convert KAN-AEs into symbolic expressions, which enables low-complexity implementation and improved energy efficiency at the radios. To further enhance the efficiency, we introduce a new non-linearity score term in the SR process to help select lower-complexity equations when possible. Through numerical simulations, we demonstrate that KAN-AEs achieve competitive BLER performance while improving energy efficiency when paired with SR. We score the energy efficiency of a KAN-AE implementation using the proposed non-linearity metric and compare it to a multi-layer perceptron-based autoencoder (MLP-AE). Our experiment shows that the KAN-AE paired with SR uses 1.38 times less energy than the MLP-AE, supporting that KAN-AEs are a promising choice for energy-efficient deep learning-based channel coding.
