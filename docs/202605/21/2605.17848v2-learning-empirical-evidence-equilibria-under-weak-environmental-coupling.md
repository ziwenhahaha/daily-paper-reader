## Abstract
Strategic multi-agent systems are fundamentally characterized by decentralization, uncertainty, and ambiguity. Agents operating under limited observations will often need to make decisions based on simplified internal models of the environment, reflecting bounded rationality in both computational capacity and environmental knowledge. The Empirical Evidence Equilibrium (EEE) framework explicitly accounts for these limitations by modeling each agent as forming a potentially misspecified belief derived from signals obtained through partial observations of the environment. The resulting equilibrium concept captures the system's steady state under bounded rationality and decentralization. In this work, we study games in which the environment dynamics are driven jointly by exogenous factors and agents' actions. We analyze agent behavior under Q-value iteration where each agent independently forms a belief model, computes Q-values, and derives a greedy strategy, yet the collective actions of all agents jointly shape the environment each agent faces at the next stage. We prove that despite this decentralization, an EEE emerges from the joint dynamics when the coupling between agents' actions and the environment is sufficiently weak. We further extend this result to softmax policies, establishing a contraction result under a sufficient coupling condition.

### 核心思想
- **EEE框架**：智能体形成可能错误的信念模型μi，基于部分观察的信号做决策，策略是最优的且信念与长期观察一致。
- **弱耦合假设**：环境转移核与一个基准核的差异有界（由耦合值λ衡量），这捕获了许多实际应用（如库存管理、渔业管理）中智能体行为对环境的影响受限于外部因素的情况。
- **分布式Q值迭代**：每个智能体独立维护Q值、信念模型和策略，但所有智能体的行动共同影响环境。

### 关键技术细节
- **随机博弈框架**：
  - 环境状态：$w \in W$，智能体状态：$x_i \in X_i$，动作：$a_i \in A_i$，信号：$s_i \in S_i$。
  - 环境动态：$w^+ \sim \Phi(w, a)$，信号生成：$s_i \sim M_i(w)$，智能体状态动态：$x_i^+ \sim \phi_i(x_i, s_i, a_i)$。
  - 阶段收益：$g_i: X_i \times A_i \times S_i \to \mathbb{R}$，折扣因子：$\delta_i$。
- **弱耦合定义**（定义2.3）：
  - 存在与动作无关的核$\Phi^U$和$\phi_i^U$，使得：
    $$\|\Phi(a) - \Phi^U\|_{r, \infty} \leq \varepsilon_\Phi, \quad \|\phi_i(x_i, s_i, a_i) - \phi_i^U(x_i, s_i)\|_{r, \infty} \leq \varepsilon_\phi,$$
    对所有$a \in A$和$i \in I$成立。
  - 总体耦合值：$\lambda = \varepsilon_\Phi + |I| \varepsilon_\phi$。
- **EEE定义**（定义2.2）：
  - **最优性**：策略$\sigma_i$相对于Q值函数是贪婪的，即在每个状态$(z_i, x_i)$选择最大化$Q_i(z_i, x_i, a_i')$的动作。
  - **一致性**：信念模型$\mu_i$与在真实环境动态$N$和策略$\sigma$下产生的长期信号频率一致：
    $$\mu_i(z_i, x_i)[s_i] = \lim_{h \to \infty} P_{N,\sigma}(s_i^h = s_i | z_i^h = z_i, x_i^h = x_i).$$
- **Q值迭代动态**（第3节）：
  - 初始化：$Q_i^{(0)}$任意，满足$\|Q_i^{(0)}\|_\infty \leq \frac{\|g_i\|_\infty}{1-\delta_i}$。
  - 每轮迭代：
    1. **贪婪策略更新**：$\sigma_i^{(t)}(z_i, x_i) \in \arg\max_{a_i \in A_i} Q_i^{(t)}(z_i, x_i, a_i)$。
    2. **一致性模型更新**：$\mu_i^{(t)}(z_i, x_i)[s_i] = \lim_{h \to \infty} P_{N,\sigma^{(t)}}(s_i^h = s_i | z_i^h = z_i, x_i^h = x_i)$。
    3. **Q值更新**：
       $$Q_i^{(t+1)}(z_i, x_i, a_i) = \sum_{s_i} \mu_i^{(t)}(z_i, x_i)[s_i] \left[ g_i(x_i, a_i, s_i) + \delta_i \mathbb{E}_{x_i^+ \sim \phi_i} V_i^{(t)}(l_i(z_i, s_i), x_i^+) \right],$$
       其中$V_i^{(t)}(z_i, x_i) = \max_{a_i} Q_i^{(t)}(z_i, x_i, a_i)$。
- **softmax策略扩展**（第3节最后）：
  - 将贪婪策略替换为softmax策略：$\sigma_i^{(t)}(z_i, x_i)[a_i] = \frac{\exp(Q_i^{(t)}(z_i, x_i, a_i)/\tau_i)}{\sum_{a_i'} \exp(Q_i^{(t)}(z_i, x_i, a_i')/\tau_i)}$，其中$\tau_i > 0$是温度参数。
  - 引入近似EEE概念（定义3.5）。

### 关键证明技术
- **命题3.3**：量化在两个不同信念模型下Q值迭代的差异上界，形式为：
  $$\lim_{t \to \infty} \|Q_i^{(t)} - \bar{Q}_i^{(t)}\|_\infty \leq \varepsilon_{i,\mu} \frac{|S_i| G_i}{(1-\delta_i)^2},$$
  其中$\varepsilon_{i,\mu}$是信念模型差异的最大值。
- **命题A.1**（附录）：在弱耦合游戏中，给定两个策略$\sigma$和$\bar{\sigma}$，信念模型差异有界：
  $$\|\mu_i(z_i, x_i) - \bar{\mu}_i(z_i, x_i)\|_\infty \leq (1 + p_{\max,i}) \frac{E_i(z_i, x_i)}{m_i} \leq (1 + p_{\max,i}) \frac{\kappa |W| |Z^{-i}| |X^{-i}| \hat{A} \|\sigma - \bar{\sigma}\|_\infty \lambda}{m_i},$$
  其中$E_i(z_i, x_i)$是联合状态分布差异，$\kappa$是Meyer条件数，$\hat{A} = \sum_{j=1}^{|I|} |A_j|$。
- **定理3.4**：在贪婪策略下，如果存在具有边缘$\xi_i > 0$的EEE，且$\lambda$足够小，则Q值迭代收敛到该EEE。
- **定理3.6**：对于softmax策略，Q值迭代在最大范数下是收缩的，收缩因子为：
  $$\rho := \max_i \left[ (1 + p_{\max,i}) \frac{\kappa |W| |Z^{-i}| |X^{-i}| \hat{A} |S_i| G_i}{m_i (1-\delta_i)} \cdot \max_i \frac{\sqrt{|A_i|}}{\tau_i} \lambda + \max_i \delta_i \right].$$
