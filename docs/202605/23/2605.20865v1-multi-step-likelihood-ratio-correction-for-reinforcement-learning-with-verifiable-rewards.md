## Abstract
Reinforcement learning with verifiable rewards (RLVR) plays a pivotal role in improving the reasoning ability of large language models. However, widely used PPO surrogate objectives are fundamentally local, as they rely on a local approximation of the exact policy gradient objective. While this approximation improves stability by reducing the variance induced by importance sampling, it also introduces structural bias into the surrogate objective, which must be controlled through trust region mechanisms. In this work, we introduce the $N$-step forward trace, which augments the PPO surrogate objective using the cumulative likelihood ratio of the next $N-1$ tokens. Building on this idea, we propose $N$-Step Forward-Trace Policy Optimization (NFPO), a practical RLVR algorithm that integrates the $N$-step forward trace into the masked policy gradient framework. NFPO provides a continuous bridge between the PPO surrogate objective and the exact policy gradient objective, offering a principled mechanism for controlling the bias-variance trade-off. Our theoretical analysis shows that, with an appropriate choice of $N$, the proposed objective yields a tighter policy-improvement bound than the standard PPO surrogate. Experiments on comprehensive reasoning benchmarks demonstrate that NFPO consistently improves performance, supporting our theoretical findings.

## 1. 核心问题与整体含义（研究动机和背景）

- **问题背景**：强化学习与可验证奖励（RLVR）是提升大语言模型（LLM）推理能力的重要范式，已被广泛应用于数学推理、代码生成等任务。

- **核心问题**：当前广泛使用的 PPO（Proximal Policy Optimization）类代理目标本质上是**局部（local）**的——它们仅基于当前 token 的似然比率（ρₜ = π(yₜ|sₜ) / μ(yₜ|sₜ)）来构造目标，而忽略了更新后策略对后续生成 token 概率的影响（即前向似然比率校正）。这种局部近似虽能降低方差、提高稳定性，却引入了结构性偏差，必须通过信任域（trust region）机制来控制。

- **研究动机**：存在一个**偏差-方差权衡（bias–variance trade-off）**——完全使用前向追踪（forward trace）是**无偏但高方差**的，而使用局部代理是**稳定但有偏**的。本文旨在提出一种能够优雅地在两者之间插值的机制。

## 2. 论文提出的方法论：核心思想、技术细节、算法流程

### 2.1 核心思想：N 步前向追踪（N-Step Forward Trace）

论文从性能差异引理（Performance Difference Lemma）出发，证明策略改进可分解为：

$$J(\pi) - J(\mu) = \mathbb{E}_{y \sim \mu}\left[ R(y) \sum_{t=1}^{T} (\rho_t - 1) \Gamma_{t+1} \right]$$

其中 $\Gamma_{t+1} = \prod_{j=t+1}^{T} \rho_j$ 称为**完整前向追踪**，它编码了后续 token 的似然比率累积效应。

### 2.2 N 步前向追踪的定义

定义**N 步前向追踪**为仅保留接下来 N−1 个 token 的似然比率：

$$\Gamma^{(N)}_{t+1} = \prod_{j=t+1}^{\min(t+N-1,T)} \rho_j$$

- **N = 1**：$\Gamma^{(1)}_{t+1} = 1$，退化为标准 PPO/GRPO 的局部代理目标
- **N = T**：$\Gamma^{(T)}_{t+1} = \Gamma_{t+1}$，恢复完整的前向追踪（精确策略梯度）

### 2.3 主要理论结果（Theorem 1）

论文证明：随着 N 增大，**截断偏差（truncation bias）**以 $O((T-N)^2 \delta^2)$ 的速率减小，但**方差**随 N 指数增长（因为涉及更多似然比率的乘积）。这为控制偏差-方差权衡提供了原则性机制。

### 2.4 NFPO 算法实现

论文提出的 **N-Step Forward-Trace Policy Optimization (NFPO)** 将 N 步前向追踪整合到掩码策略梯度框架中，具体实现包括：

1. **似然比率裁剪**：$\rho_{t,\beta} = \text{clip}(\rho_t, 1/\beta, \beta)$，防止极端值
2. **前向追踪裁剪**：$\bar{\Gamma}^{(N)}_{t+1} = \text{clip}\left(\prod_{j=t+1}^{t+N-1} \rho_{j,\beta}, 1-\epsilon_{\text{low}}, 1+\epsilon_{\text{high}}\right)$
3. **信任域**：采用 DPPO 的总变差（TV）token 掩码，$M_t = \mathbf{1}\left[ \text{DTV}(\mu(\cdot|s_t), \pi(\cdot|s_t)) \leq \delta \text{ or } A_t(\rho_t - 1) \leq 0 \right]$
4. **损失函数**：$\mathcal{L}_{\text{NFPO}} = \frac{1}{G} \sum_{i=1}^{G} \sum_{t=1}^{T} M^{(i)}_t \cdot A^{(i)}_t \cdot \rho^{(i)}_t \cdot \bar{\Gamma}^{(N,i)}_{t+1}$

   - 1.7B 模型：N=4 最优
   - 8B 模型：N=8 最优

4. **理论验证**：Theorem 1 从理论上证实了 N 步前向追踪的偏差-方差权衡，为实践提供了原则性指导
