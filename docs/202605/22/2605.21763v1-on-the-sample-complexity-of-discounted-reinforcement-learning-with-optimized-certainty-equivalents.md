## Abstract
We study risk-sensitive reinforcement learning in finite discounted MDPs, where a generative model of the MDP is assumed to be available. We consider a family or risk measures called the optimized certainty equivalent (OCE), which includes important risk measures such as entropic risk, CVaR, and mean-variance. Our focus is on the sample complexities of learning the optimal state-action value function (value learning) and an optimal policy (policy learning) under recursive OCE. We provide an exact characterization of utility functions $u$ for which the corresponding OCE defines an objective that is PAC-learnable. We analyze a simple model-based approach and derive PAC sample complexity bounds. We establish that whenever $u$ does not have full domain $\text{dom}(u)\neq \mathbb{R}$, the corresponding problem is not PAC-learnable. Finally, we establish corresponding lower bounds for both value and policy learning, demonstrating tightness in the size $SA$ of state-action space, and for a more restricted class of utilities, we derive lower bounds that makes the dependence on the effective horizon $\frac{1}{1-γ}$ explicit. Specifically, for $\text{CVaR}_τ$ we show that the correct dependence on $τ$ is $\frac{1}{τ^2}$, thus improving by a factor of $\frac{1}τ$ over state-of-the-art although our bound has a suboptimal dependence on $\frac{1}{1-γ}$.

## 2. 论文提出的方法论

### 核心思想
将**模型-based RL**方法扩展到递归OCE目标，通过在经验MDP上执行值迭代来学习最优Q函数或最优策略。

### 关键技术细节

#### 2.1 OCE定义
OCE基于utility函数 $u: \mathbb{R} \to [-\infty, \infty)$，需满足：
- 闭合、适当、凹、非递减
- $u(0) = 0$ 且 $1 \in \partial u(0)$
- $\text{dom}(u) = \{t \in \mathbb{R} | u(t) > -\infty\}$

**OCE表达式**：
$$\text{OCE}_u(X) = \sup_{\eta \in \mathbb{R}} (\eta + \mathbb{E}[u(X - \eta)])$$

**关键函数类**：
- $U_0$：满足上述条件的utility函数集合
- $U_1 = \{u \in U_0 | \text{dom}(u) = \mathbb{R}\}$：**全定义域**utility函数（本文主要研究对象）
- $U_<^1$：强风险厌恶utility函数

#### 2.2 递归OCE目标
价值函数和Q函数通过OCE递归定义：
$$V^\pi(s_0) = r_0 + \gamma\rho_{s_0,\pi(s_0)}(r_1 + \gamma\rho_{s_1,\pi(s_1)}(r_2 + \gamma\rho_{s_2,\pi(s_2)}(\cdots)))$$
$$Q^\pi(s_0, a) = R(s_0, a) + \gamma\rho_{s_0,a}(\max_{a'} Q^\pi(s', a'))$$

#### 2.3 贝尔曼最优算子
$$\text{OCE-VI算子：} (Tf)(s,a) = R(s,a) + \gamma\rho_{s,a}(\max_{a'} f(s',a'))$$

**收缩性**：$T$ 是关于$\|\cdot\|_\infty$的$\gamma$-收缩映射，因此值迭代收敛。

#### 2.4 算法流程：MB-OCE-VI

**步骤1-模型估计**：
- 对每个状态-动作对$(s,a)$，收集$N$个转移样本
- 估计经验转移概率 $\hat{P}(s'|s,a) = \frac{n(s,a,s')}{N}$
- 构建经验MDP $\tilde{M} = (S, A, \hat{P}, R, \gamma)$

**步骤2-值迭代**：
- 在经验MDP上执行$Q$-值迭代
- 迭代次数 $k \geq \frac{\log(\frac{1}{2\epsilon(1-\gamma)})}{\log(1/\gamma)}$ 确保收敛

**步骤3-输出**：
- 返回 $Q_k$ 和贪婪策略 $\pi_k$

### 关键理论结果

#### 定理1（PAC上界）
若 $u \in U_1$ 且样本数 $T$ 满足：
$$T \geq \frac{32\gamma^2 SA [u(-H_\gamma)]^2}{\epsilon^2(1-\gamma)^2} \log\left(\frac{8\gamma SA u'_+(-\frac{\gamma}{1-\gamma})}{\epsilon(1-\gamma)^2}\right)$$
则算法以高概率输出近似最优Q函数。

#### 引理1（扰动敏感性）
刻画了Q函数对转移概率扰动的敏感度上界：
$$\|Q^\pi - \tilde{Q}^\pi\| \leq \frac{\gamma}{1-\gamma} \max_{s,a} \sup_{\eta \in [0, H_\gamma]} \sum_{s'} [P_{s,a}(s') - \tilde{P}_{s,a}(s')] u(V^\pi(s') - \eta)$$

## 6. 论文的主要结论与发现

### 核心结论1：PAC可学习性的精确刻画
> **充要条件**：对于递归OCE目标，当且仅当utility函数$u$属于$U_1$（即$\text{dom}(u) = \mathbb{R}$）时，相应的学习问题是PAC可学习的。

- 若$\text{dom}(u) \neq \mathbb{R}$，存在构造的MDP使得任何学习算法都无法获得PAC保证

### 核心结论2：MB-OCE-VI的样本复杂度
| 风险度量 | 策略学习样本复杂度 |
|----------|-------------------|
| 熵风险（β>0） | $\tilde{O}\left(\frac{SA}{\epsilon^2(1-\gamma)^5} \beta^2 \left(e^{\frac{\beta}{1-\gamma}}-1\right)^2\right)$ |
| CVaR_τ | $\tilde{O}\left(\frac{SA}{\epsilon^2(1-\gamma)^6 \tau^2}\right)$ |
| 均值-方差 | $\tilde{O}\left(\frac{SA}{\epsilon^2(1-\gamma)^8}\right)$ |

### 核心结论3：CVaR的τ依赖性改进
- 现有最优下界：$\tilde{\Omega}\left(\frac{SA}{\epsilon^2(1-\gamma)^4\tau}\right)$
- 本文证明的紧下界：$\tilde{\Omega}\left(\frac{SA}{\epsilon^2(1-\gamma)^2\tau^2}\right)$
- **改进**：在τ很小时（τ ≪ 1/(1-γ)），本文下界更优

### 核心结论4：上下界匹配
- 上界和下界在$S, A, 1/\epsilon, 1/\delta$维度上匹配
- 但在有效视界$1/(1-\gamma)$的依赖上存在差距

- 离散化过程可能需要$O(u'_+(-\frac{\gamma}{1-\gamma})/\epsilon)$个点，实际计算开销可能较大
