## Abstract
Reinforcement learning algorithms are generally designed to maximize the expected return across a population. However, a policy that is optimal on average may be suboptimal for certain individuals, leading to potential safety concerns. To address this, we first formalize the notion of individual harm from a counterfactual perspective and define harm as the event in which a chosen action results in a strictly worse outcome than a baseline alternative. We then propose a general two-stage procedure for learning policies that maximize the expected return while accounting for individual harm. We further establish the finite-sample properties of the learned policy, derive an upper bound on its sub-optimality gap, and show that the harm rate remains well-controlled. Numerical experiments on both simulated and real-world datasets demonstrate the effectiveness of the proposed approach.

## 2. 论文方法论

**核心思想：**
- 将反事实伤害量化后纳入效用函数，通过惩罚机制平衡效果与安全
- 效用函数定义为：$R_t(a) = Y_t(a) - \beta \cdot HR(X_t; a, a')$，其中 $\beta$ 为风险规避因子

**关键技术细节：**

### 2.1 识别假设
- **假设1（数据生成过程）**：马尔可夫性、条件均值独立、平稳性
- **假设2（无未测量混杂）**：$X_t$ 阻塞所有从 $A_t$ 到 $X_{t+1}$ 和 $Y_t$ 的后门路径
- **假设3（Copula模型）**：利用高斯Copula建模潜在结果间的相关性，在给定协变量下，伤害率可识别为：
  $$HR(x; a, a') = \Phi\left(\frac{r(x,a') - r(x,a)}{\sqrt{\sigma^2(x,a) + \sigma^2(x,a') - 2\rho\sigma(x,a)\sigma(x,a')}}\right)$$

### 2.2 两阶段算法流程

**第一阶段（Algorithm 1 - 效用估计）：**
1. 输入数据集 $D = \{(X_{it}, A_{it}, Y_{it})\}_{i=1}^N$
2. 估计 $r(x,a)$（条件均值）和 $\sigma(x,a)$（条件标准差）
3. 构建伪效用：$\tilde{R}_{it} = Y_{it} - \beta \cdot \widehat{HR}(X_{it}; A_{it}, a')$
4. 输出变换后的数据集 $\tilde{D}$

**第二阶段（Algorithm 2 - Fitted Q-Iteration）：**
1. 将数据展平为 $(X_i, A_i, \tilde{R}_i, X_i')$ 形式的合并数据集
2. 迭代K次，每次计算目标 $R_i^{(k)} \leftarrow \tilde{R}_i + \gamma \max_{a'} Q_{k-1}(X_i', a')$
3. 通过最小化均方误差拟合新的Q函数
4. 返回策略 $\pi_{\tilde{Q}_K}(x) = \arg\max_a \tilde{Q}_K(x,a)$

### 2.3 理论保证
- **定理1（遗憾界）**：$|J(\pi^*) - J(\pi_{\tilde{Q}_K})|$ 受三部分上界控制：
  1. 伤害率估计误差（与 $\epsilon_n$ 成正比）
  2. FQI一步估计误差（$O(\log n / \sqrt{n})$）
  3. 初始化偏差（随K指数衰减）
- **定理2（伤害界）**：在边际条件（Margin Condition）下，额外伤害以 $O(((1-\gamma)R_n)^{\alpha/(\alpha+1)})$ 收敛

   - Harm-aware方法将伤害率降至约0.065-0.13（约降低70%）
   - 同时保持相近的总体疗效

5. **理论贡献**：建立了有限样本后悔界和伤害界，证明方法可渐近达到最优安全水平
